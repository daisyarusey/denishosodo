Manifesto

ITS ALL ABOUT YOU!

Member Protection
  • Creation of a subscription amnesty framework that provides members a grace period to pay premiums in cases of financial difficulties and major world disruptions such as the Covid Pandemic.
  • Enhance affordability of trainings and provide more benefits to ensure CPAs get value for their investment.
  • Development of online CPD courses and partnering with other learning institutions such as LinkedIn.
Strengthening the CPA (K) brand
  • Ensure accountancy, audit and tax services are conducted by ICPAK Members only.
  • Job hunting for ICPAK Members locally and abroad. E.g CPA Australia, USA etc.
  • Pursue conversion of CPA qualification to Internationally recognised certifications like ACCA, ACA, CA.
  • Pursue MOUs with universities and colleges for credit transfers for CPA graduates.
Enhancing Efficiency and sustainability of the Institute
  • Diversifying revenue earning channels for the Institutte
  • Amplifying the use of technology to running of the institute and provision of member services
  • Enhance CPA curriculum to fit the needs of the current digital world and future proof the profession.
Advocating for Financial Accountability in the Country
  • Spearhead financial accountability in government institutions and Counties by highlighting mismanagement and amplifying a call for action to the responsible arms of government.
  • Increase the Institute's visibility in matters of National Interest like Debt Crisis, Devolution and the state of the Economy.
  • Champion campaigns to hold government and county institutions accountable.
